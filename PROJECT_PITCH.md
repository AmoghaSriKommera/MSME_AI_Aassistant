# MSME AI Assistant Project Pitch

## Problem Statement

Indian MSMEs often struggle to identify relevant schemes, understand
eligibility conditions, and navigate application processes.

## Proposed Solution

An AI-powered assistant that acts as a digital advisor for MSMEs using
retrieval augmented generation and agentic workflows.

## Unique Selling Points

-   Multi-agent intelligence
-   Eligibility reasoning
-   Document extraction
-   Scheme comparison
-   Conversational guidance

## Expected Impact

Improve awareness and accessibility of government schemes for MSMEs.
